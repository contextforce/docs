## Integrate AI into your workflow

To integrate AI into your workflow, we've created the following:
* Quick menu for selected text
* Image menu when you mouse over an image
* Youtube summarize button
* Context capture from youtube, google search result and webpage so you can ask questions about the content.
* Screenshot capture certain area of the page and ask questions about the screenshot.
* Shortcut to open LEXI sidebar
* Chat Editor shortcuts: `/` to open prompt menu, `@` to show memos, and `#` to show tags.

> We will continue to add more integration points to make AI more seamless and integrated into your daily workflow!
